Software Engineer Job Requirements

Job Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
  • Proficiency in one or more programming languages (e.g., Java, Python, C++).
  • Strong software development skills, including design, coding, and testing.
  • Knowledge of data structures and algorithms.
  • Excellent problem-solving and debugging skills.
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team.

Qualifications

  • 2+ years of experience in software development.
  • Experience with front-end or back-end development, depending on the role.
  • Strong knowledge of software engineering best practices.
  • Familiarity with version control systems (e.g., Git).
