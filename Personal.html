My Hero/Heros
My strongest traits are having a creative mind and being observant. Me having a creative mind really does have a positive and maybe a downside to it at times. For instance, when given a task I tend to quickly start brainstorming several ideas on how I’ll go about that specific task but the downside to it is that while I am thinking about possible solutions my focus will be diverted from everything else. A perfect example would be in a classroom setting. Persons who I look up as role models and inspirations are my aunts on Mother side of my family. Being around them and observing how hard they worked to be where they’re at right now really influenced me a lot to work harder and go after my dreams. And the support and expectation which is received from them really pushes me. I am worried about the thought of letting them and my lady down.
